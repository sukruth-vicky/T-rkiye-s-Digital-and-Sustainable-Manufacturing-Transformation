Türkiye’s Digital and Sustainable Manufacturing Transformation
A Case Study Analysis Inspired by McKinsey and Company

by Sukruth M

Overview

This project analyzes McKinsey and Company’s case study on how Türkiye is transforming its manufacturing sector through digital innovation and sustainability. 
Supported by MESS and MEXT, the initiative focuses on capability building, Industry 4.0 adoption, and long-term operational resilience.
